Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: 13 July 2026 · Version 1.0

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from Bharat Resume under §12 of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. This page tells you exactly how to make the request, what will be deleted, what will be retained, and how long it will take.

Fast path: email help.resumebharat@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request", or WhatsApp / call +91 63673 77638. We will confirm within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.

1. How to make a request

Send an email to help.resumebharat@gmail.com including all of the following:

  1. Subject line: Data Deletion Request
  2. Your full name (as provided to Bharat Resume).
  3. The WhatsApp phone number you used to interact with the Service, in international format (for example, +91 98765 43210).
  4. The email address you provided in the resume flow, if known.
  5. An approximate date of your last use of the Service.
  6. A confirmation statement: "I confirm that this deletion request is made by me or by a person authorised on my behalf."

If you are unable to send email, you may alternatively send this information as a WhatsApp message to Bharat Resume from the same phone number, including the phrase "data deletion request".

3. What we cannot delete (and why)

Certain records must be retained even after a deletion request, to comply with Indian law:

  • Payment records — retained for 8 years as required under §44AA of the Income-tax Act, 1961 for book-keeping and audit. These records contain only the payment amount, payment identifier and hashed reference; they do not contain your resume content.
  • Fraud, dispute, and abuse investigation records — retained for the duration of the investigation, and thereafter for the period required by law.
  • Copies of correspondence you have sent to us that we are legally required to retain — for example, communications relating to a formal legal notice or a regulatory investigation.

The DPDP Act, 2023 §12(3) explicitly permits a Data Fiduciary to retain personal data notwithstanding a deletion request where retention is required by law or is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. We rely on this provision only where clearly applicable.
