9. AI-generated content — how to interpret the output

Our AI rewrites your inputs into resume-appropriate language. In doing so it may (i) tighten grammar, (ii) reorganise bullets for impact, (iii) apply an implicit-responsibility carve-out — writing a qualitative bullet describing a duty that is unambiguously implied by the role you claim (for example, "coordinated logistics" for someone who states they led a large event). The AI is instructed never to invent numbers, tools, employers, dates, or outcomes you did not state. Notwithstanding this, we do not guarantee that every generated sentence is a verbatim reflection of your input, and you are expected to review the delivered PDF before sharing it.

The ATS Score displayed inside the conversation is a proxy computed by our deterministic scorer. It is not the score of any specific recruiter's applicant-tracking system, and it does not guarantee that your resume will be selected by any hiring process. We make no representation about hiring outcomes.
